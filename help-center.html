Logistics HELP_01

Orders & Delivery

How long does delivery take?
Digital items are typically delivered within 5-30 minutes after payment verification. Some specialized gaming accounts may take up to 2 hours for security auditing.
Where do I find my credentials?
Your account details are sent to the email address provided during checkout. Please check your "Spam" or "Promotions" folder if you don't see it in your Inbox.
Finance HELP_02

Payments & Billing

Which payment methods are accepted?

To keep our prices competitive, we exclusively accept the following manual payment methods:

  • Bank Transfer: Standard bank deposit or online transfer (LankaPay).
  • eZ Cash: Mobile wallet transfer via Dialog.

Note: After transfer, you must send a clear screenshot of the receipt to our WhatsApp support for verification.

How do I verify my Bank/eZ Cash transfer?
Once you have made the payment, please contact our support desk on WhatsApp with your Order ID and a screenshot of the payment receipt. Our team will verify the transaction and release your digital product instantly.
Identity HELP_03

Account Security

Can I change the email on my new account?
For most full-access accounts, yes. However, we recommend waiting 24-48 hours after receiving the credentials before making major security changes to avoid triggering platform security flags.
