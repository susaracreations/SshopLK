Frequently Asked Questions

How do I make a purchase?

Browse our products, select the item you want, and click "Buy Now". You'll be redirected to contact our support team via WhatsApp or Discord to complete your purchase securely.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including bank transfers, mobile payments, and digital wallets. Payment details will be provided during the purchase process.

How long does delivery take?

Digital products are typically delivered instantly or within 24 hours. Physical items may take 3-7 business days depending on your location.

What if I have issues with my purchase?

Contact our support team immediately via WhatsApp or Discord. We provide 14-day insurance on all purchases and will assist you with any issues.

Are your products legitimate?

Yes, all our products are legitimate and sourced from authorized distributors. We are a trusted seller with over 1000 successful sales and provide insurance on all purchases.

Can I get a refund?

Refunds are handled on a case-by-case basis. If you experience issues with your purchase, contact our support team within 14 days for assistance.

How do I create an account?

Click "Sign In" in the header, then "Sign Up" to create your account. You can browse products without an account, but creating one allows you to track orders and save preferences.

What regions do you serve?

We primarily serve Sri Lanka and surrounding regions. Some products may have regional restrictions, which will be clearly indicated on the product page.
